Acere, que bola?!

The caribbean island of Cuba is characterized by its colorful spanish-colonia style architecture, salsa music, tobacco plantations, white sand beaches and wonderful people. Make some friends, find a street party, enjoy a mojito and dance to some live music. This beautiful country is full of history, full of life and full of suprises

Wander and Discover. There are a variety of things to do and explore, with people more than willing to help you along the way and share their life and culture
